Free Online Assistive Tools To Help
With Learning, Communication and Organization


Learning disabilities are neurologically-based processing problems. These processing problems can interfere with learning basic skills such as reading, writing and communication. We are earmarking our web application for 20% of the World’s population facing AUDITORY PROCESSING DISORDER(ADP) and between 5-10% of the population facing DYSLEXIA.


Assistive Technology – a generic name associated with devices and programs used for people with disabilities, is playing an essential role in the world. This type of technology helps disable people accomplish tasks, almost impossible before, encouraging them to be a significant part of society. Our web application initiates classroom support and features to help with auditory processing disorder and dyslexia accordingly.
